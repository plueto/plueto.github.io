For Filmmakers

Plueto TV is a curated streaming platform focused on cult cinema, retro sci-fi, experimental animation, psychological thrillers, and reality-bending films.

Our goal is to spotlight independent creators and films that push the boundaries of storytelling.

What We Offer

  • Curated placement on the platform
  • Dedicated watch page
  • Exposure to a niche film audience
  • Revenue share when monetization launches
  • Non-exclusive distribution

What We're Looking For

  • Cult cinema
  • Retro sci-fi
  • Experimental animation
  • Reality-bending stories
  • Psychological thrillers
  • Short films and indie features
